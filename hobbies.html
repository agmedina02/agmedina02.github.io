Hobbies

When I'm not busy with schoolwork, I like to take a break by dancing hip hop for an organization on campus called Dancin' Gators. It's a fun form of exercise for me and helps to clear my mind of the clutter that I may have accumilated throughout the week.

It's probably not a surprise that as a jouralism major that I love to read anything I can get my hands on whether it's a magazine or one of my favorite young adult novels. Even during my free time I'm still writing, though it's usually in the form of a short story or written letter to relative.

If I'm feeling especially creative I break out my art supplies from their hidden place to start a new do it yourself project. I'm fairly new to this world and I usally get most of my ideas from Pinterest or watching one of my favorite vloggers on YouTube. Most of my creations are usually made for my friends or family.

If I'm not doing any of those activities above then you'll more than likely find me at the nearest mall dreaming of a purse I'll never be able to afford or anywhere there is free food.
